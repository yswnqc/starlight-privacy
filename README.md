# 星星点灯 · Starlight

> 埋头努力，也不要忘了仰望星空。
> Even buried in the grind, don't forget to look up at the stars.

**Starlight** is a calm, minimal habit tracker. Every habit you keep is a "river of stars" in your
night sky — each night you show up, you light a star by hand. Over time your effort becomes a sky
you can look up to.

**星星点灯** 是一款极简的习惯打卡 app：你做的每件小事都是夜空里的一条「星河」，每坚持一夜就点亮一颗星，日子久了，那片星空全是你认真过的夜晚。
